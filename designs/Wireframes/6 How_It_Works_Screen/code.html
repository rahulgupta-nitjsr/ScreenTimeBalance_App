How It Works

Earning Time

You earn screen time by logging healthy habits. Each habit is worth a certain amount of time, and you can earn up to 2 hours per day.

Habits

We've curated a list of science-backed habits that promote well-being. You can choose which habits to track, and we'll help you stay consistent.

Penalties

If you exceed your daily screen time limit, you'll lose time from your balance. This helps you stay mindful of your usage.

Why This Works

Our approach is based on behavioral science principles like positive reinforcement and habit formation. We focus on rewarding healthy choices, not punishing screen time.
